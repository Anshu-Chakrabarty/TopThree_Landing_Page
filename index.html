Our Process

Not an algorithm.
Real humans. Real research.

Unlike platforms where agents pay for placement, our team evaluates every realtor personally — on their sales record, client reviews, licensing, and local expertise.

No sponsored listings — ever

Rankings are earned, not bought. No realtor can pay to appear in our top three. Revenue comes only from referrals after a match.

50-point manual inspection

We verify licenses, cross-check MLS sales records, and assess client satisfaction across dozens of signals. No algorithm can replicate this.

Refreshed every 6 months

Real estate markets move fast. We re-vet every agent twice a year so our rankings always reflect current performance, not old glory.
